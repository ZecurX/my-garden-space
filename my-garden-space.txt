My Garden Space – Website Requirements Document
1. Project Overview
Project Name: My Garden Space​
Business Type: Garden & Landscaping Services + Plant Rental​
Objective: Build a professional, user-friendly website to showcase gardening and
landscaping services, enable plant rentals, generate service inquiries, and build trust
with customers.
The website should help convert visitors into leads, allow easy service discovery, and
support future scalability
